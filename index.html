Dreamcore/Weirdcore

a surrealist aesthetic




Dreamcore is a surrealist aesthetic that uses motifs commonly associated with dreams, daydreams or nightmares, portrayed through media such as images, videos and, on occasion, music. Dreamcore shares many similarities with weirdcore; however, it was not made to give you a dread and uneasy feeling Instead, dreamcore focuses on emulating the general feeling of a dream and uses brighter color
Weirdcore is a surrealist aesthetic centered around amateur or low-quality photography and/or visual images that have been constructed or edited to convey feelings of confusion, disorientation, dread, alienation, and nostalgia or anemoia.
